NEUROPSYCH

The study of the relationship between behavior, emotion, and cognition on the one hand, and brain function on the other.

A general umbrella term that refers to the brain's ability to modify, change, and adapt both structure and function throughout life and in response to experience.

NEUROPLASTICITY

Neurotransmitters are the messengers of the nervous system. They are relatively small molecules that carry information across synapses from a nerve cell to its neighboring cells.
